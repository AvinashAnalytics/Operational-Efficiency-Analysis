    <h2>Project Overview</h2>
    <p>This project aims to improve operational efficiency and employee engagement in a multi-departmental business. By leveraging data analysis techniques, the goal is to optimize decision-making processes related to employee performance, product sales, customer satisfaction, and inventory management using SQL and business intelligence tools.</p>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h2>Problem Statement</h2>
    <ul>
      <li><b>Employee Performance & Retention:</b> High turnover rates and inconsistent performance impact productivity.</li>
      <li><b>Product Sales & Customer Satisfaction:</b> Low customer feedback despite high sales, indicating quality or support issues.</li>
      <li><b>Supply Chain & Inventory Management:</b> Low inventory levels and supply chain risks that may lead to missed opportunities.</li>
      <li><b>Technology Usage & Future Planning:</b> Underutilization of technology and weak market research affects competitiveness.</li>
    </ul>
  </section>

  <section>
    <h2>Key Analysis Questions</h2>
    <ul>
      <li><b>Employee Performance:</b> Which department had the highest average profit margin?</li>
      <li><b>Product Sales & Customer Satisfaction:</b> Identify the product with the highest revenue in the HR department.</li>
      <li><b>Supply Chain & Inventory Management:</b> Which supplier had the highest total inventory level?</li>
      <li><b>Employee Training & Sales:</b> How many employees in the Sales department have completed training programs?</li>
      <li><b>Advanced SQL Questions:</b> Including window functions, stored procedures, and triggers.</li>
    </ul>
  </section>
